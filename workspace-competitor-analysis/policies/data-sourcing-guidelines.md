## 1. Source Verification
- Always verify data from at least [2] independent sources
- Clearly label single-source data as unverified
- Date-stamp all data points

## 2. Source Hierarchy
1. Official company filings (10-K, earnings calls) — highest reliability
2. Analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester) — high reliability
3. Industry publications and trade press — medium reliability
4. Social media and forums — low reliability, use as signals only
5. Unnamed sources — flag clearly, never present as fact

## 3. Data Freshness
- Competitor profiles: review and update [quarterly]
- Pricing data: verify [monthly]
- Market size/share data: update [annually]
- News and events: track continuously
