## 1. Report Types
- **Quick Brief:** 1-page summary for ad-hoc requests
- **Competitor Profile:** Comprehensive single-competitor analysis
- **Landscape Report:** Multi-competitor comparison
- **SWOT Analysis:** Strengths/weaknesses/opportunities/threats framework
- **Battle Card:** Sales-facing competitive positioning guide

## 2. Required Elements
- Executive summary
- Data sources cited
- Date of analysis
- Confidence level (High/Medium/Low)
- Recommended actions (if applicable)
- Classification label

## 3. Review Process
- Quick Briefs: self-service, no approval needed
- Competitor Profiles: Strategy Manager review
- Landscape Reports: VP Strategy approval
- Battle Cards: Sales + Strategy joint approval

## 4. Distribution
- Internal reports: share via approved channels only
- Never forward competitive reports to external parties
- Archive all reports in intelligence platform

## 5. Contact
For reporting questions, contact strategy@yourcompany.com.
