## 1. Ethical Standards
- All intelligence gathered through legal, ethical, public sources only
- No social engineering, impersonation, or deception
- No reverse engineering of competitor IP without Legal approval
- Respect competitor confidentiality agreements

## 2. Approved Sources
- Public websites, press releases, and SEC filings
- Industry analyst reports (Gartner, Forrester, IDC)
- Trade shows and conferences
- Published customer reviews and case studies
- Job postings and LinkedIn profiles (public data only)
- Patent databases (USPTO, EPO)

## 3. Prohibited Activities
- Hiring competitor employees solely for proprietary information
- Accessing non-public competitor systems or data
- Using deceptive practices to obtain information
- Sharing false information about competitors

## 4. Data Classification
- **Public:** Freely sharable within the company
- **Internal:** Share within strategy and sales teams only
- **Confidential:** Strategy leadership only

## 5. Contact
For competitive intelligence questions, contact strategy@yourcompany.com.
