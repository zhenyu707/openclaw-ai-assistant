# Financial Policy Q&A

## When to Use
When an employee asks about financial policies: expenses, travel, procurement, budgets, etc.

## Policy Document Locations
- Expense policy: policies/expense-policy.md
- Procurement policy: policies/procurement-policy.md
- Travel policy: policies/travel-policy.md
- Budget guidelines: policies/budget-guidelines.md

## Steps

1. Identify the policy topic from the employee's question
2. Use the read tool to load the relevant policy document
3. Extract the specific relevant section
4. Provide a clear, concise answer citing the policy
5. Offer to escalate to Finance Manager if the question needs interpretation

## Important
- Always cite which policy document you're quoting from
- Do NOT interpret policies in ways that change their meaning
- When in doubt, say: "I'd recommend confirming this with the Finance team"
- If a policy document doesn't exist yet, say: "That policy document is not yet available. Please contact Finance directly."
