## 1. Receiving Process
1. Check delivery against PO number
2. Verify quantity matches PO and packing slip
3. Inspect for visible damage
4. Record condition on receiving form
5. Accept or reject delivery

## 2. Inspection Levels
- **Standard:** Visual inspection + count verification
- **Detailed:** Sample testing for quality-sensitive items
- **Full:** 100% inspection for high-value or first-time suppliers

## 3. Discrepancy Handling
- Short shipment: note on BOL, notify supplier within [24]hr
- Overage: accept and notify supplier, or refuse excess
- Damaged goods: photograph, reject, file claim within [48]hr
- Wrong items: reject, notify supplier immediately

## 4. Documentation
- All receiving logged in inventory system
- Photos of damage retained for [90] days
- Inspection reports filed with PO documentation

## 5. Contact
For receiving questions, contact warehouse@yourcompany.com.
