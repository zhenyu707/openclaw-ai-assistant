# Inventory Policy Q&A

## When to Use
When someone asks about inventory SOPs, reorder policies, warehouse procedures, or receiving guidelines.

## Policy Document Locations
- Inventory SOP: policies/inventory-sop.md
- Reorder policy: policies/reorder-policy.md
- Warehouse procedures: policies/warehouse-procedures.md
- Receiving/inspection: policies/receiving-inspection-guidelines.md

## Steps
1. Identify the topic
2. Read the relevant policy document
3. Extract the specific section
4. Provide a clear answer citing the policy
5. Offer to escalate to Inventory Manager if needed

## Important
- Always cite which document you're quoting from
- Do NOT interpret SOPs in ways that change their meaning
- When in doubt: "I'd recommend confirming with the Inventory Manager"
