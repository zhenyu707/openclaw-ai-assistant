# Operations Policy Q&A

## When to Use
When someone asks about operations SOPs, facility guidelines, vendor policies, or incident procedures.

## Policy Document Locations
- Operations SOP: policies/operations-sop.md
- Vendor management: policies/vendor-management-policy.md
- Facility guidelines: policies/facility-guidelines.md
- Incident response: policies/incident-response-process.md

## Steps
1. Identify the topic
2. Read the relevant policy document
3. Extract the specific section
4. Provide a clear answer citing the policy
5. Offer to escalate to Operations Manager if needed

## Important
- Always cite which document you're quoting from
- Do NOT interpret SOPs in ways that change their meaning
- When in doubt: "I'd recommend confirming with the Operations Manager"
