## 1. Our Commitment
We are committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and ethical workplace where all employees
can thrive. This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and representatives of [Company Name].

## 2. Expected Behavior
- Treat all colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and professionalism
- Communicate openly and honestly
- Protect confidential company and customer information
- Follow all applicable laws and regulations
- Report concerns through appropriate channels

## 3. Prohibited Conduct
- **Harassment:** Any unwanted conduct based on race, gender, age, religion, disability, or other
  protected characteristic — verbal, physical, or digital
- **Discrimination:** Unfair treatment of any individual based on protected characteristics
- **Retaliation:** Any adverse action against an employee for reporting a concern in good faith
- **Conflicts of interest:** Undisclosed personal interests that conflict with company interests
- **Misuse of company resources:** Using company assets for personal benefit without authorization
- **Fraud or dishonesty:** Falsifying records, misrepresenting facts, or theft

## 4. Conflicts of Interest
- Disclose any potential conflict of interest to your manager and HR
- Examples: outside employment, investments in competitors, personal relationships with vendors
- HR will determine whether a conflict exists and how to manage it

## 5. Confidentiality
- Do not share proprietary, financial, or customer data with unauthorized parties
- Confidentiality obligations survive employment termination
- Refer to your NDA and data handling policies for specifics

## 6. Reporting Concerns
- **Manager:** First point of contact for most workplace concerns
- **HR:** hr@yourcompany.com or HR Bot (for anonymous reporting, use [ethics hotline])
- **Legal:** legal@yourcompany.com for potential legal/compliance issues
- All reports are treated confidentially; retaliation is strictly prohibited

## 7. Disciplinary Process
1. Investigation of reported concern
2. Finding communicated to relevant parties
3. Corrective action if warranted: verbal warning → written warning → PIP → termination
4. Appeals process available; contact HR Director

## 8. Acknowledgment
All employees must acknowledge this policy annually. Contact HR if you have questions.
