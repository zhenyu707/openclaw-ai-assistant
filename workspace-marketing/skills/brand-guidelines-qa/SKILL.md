# Brand Guidelines Q&A

## When to Use
When someone asks about brand guidelines, content standards, or marketing policies.

## Policy Document Locations
- Brand guidelines: policies/brand-guidelines.md
- Social media policy: policies/social-media-policy.md
- Content standards: policies/content-standards.md
- Campaign approval process: policies/campaign-approval-process.md

## Steps
1. Identify the topic from the question
2. Read the relevant policy document
3. Extract the specific relevant section
4. Provide a clear answer citing the policy
5. Offer to escalate to Marketing Manager if interpretation needed

## Important
- Always cite which document you're quoting from
- Do NOT interpret guidelines in ways that change their meaning
- When in doubt: "I'd recommend confirming with the Marketing Manager"
